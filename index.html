About Me

My name is Jonathan and I am a PhD candidate at the UCR Department of Mathematics. I received my Bachelor's degree in pure mathematics in June 2016, my Master's degree in Fall 2018, and plan to graduate in Spring 2022.

My research interests include Low-Dimensional Topology and Geometric Group Theory. My thesis advisor is Dr. Stefano Vidussi.

At UCR, I am a Graduate Recruitment Ambassador, Vice President of the UCR student chapter of the American Mathematics Society, and co-founder for the UCR student chapter of the Pacific Math Alliance.
