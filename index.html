About Me

My name is Jonathan and I am a fourth year graduate student at the UCR Department of Mathematics. I received my Bachelor's degree in pure mathematics in June 2016 and my Master's degree in January 2019.

My research interests include Low-Dimensional Topology and Geometric Group Theory. My thesis advisor is Dr. Stefano Vidussi.

At UCR, I am a Graduate Recruitment Ambassador and the Vice President of the UCR student chapter of the American Mathematics Society.
